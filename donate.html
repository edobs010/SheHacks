Donate

Below are several great oppritunities for you to make a difference. Explore different companies and find which of their goals align with your own.

a living tribute

A living tribute allows you to plant a tree in memory of someone or gift someone a tree within Canada. You can choose from various locations and plant between one and 500 trees, prices start at $25.00.

ONETREEPLANTED

For just a dollar a tree One Tree Planted will plant trees where they are needed most. You can visit their website below to see where they are actively planting. Through One Tree Planted you can also set up recurring donations to continue your efforts.

Forests Ontario
Forests Ontario is a non-profit charity that relies on donations for their programs. You can donate between $10.00 and $500.00 as a one time payment or as a recurring donation.
World Land Trust
The World Land Trust not only plants trees but also protects and preserves them. They are an international conservation charity with a focus on threatened habitats and endangered species.
